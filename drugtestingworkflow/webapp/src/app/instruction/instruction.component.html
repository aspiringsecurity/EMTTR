Instruction

  1. Ensure that you have a token in the account. If you don't have on this demo, claiming is available on the top of screen.
  2. Add a drug task to the ecosytem, you will need to deposit some amount of token into an escrow contract.
  3. Switch the account on metamask, pick the task and put the drug efficacy in the form on the item detail page.
  4. Drug owner need to validate the result one by one. Then can click award button on the screen.
  5. Reward will be released from a smart contract to the best team.
